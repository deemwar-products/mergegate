## Finding 1 â€” bundled `deemwar-agent` pattern is stale (HIGH)

The registry's `deemwar-agent` entry matches only `agents@deemwar.com`
(`src/agents.ts:52`). The live fleet commits under **per-line identities** â€”
`io@`, `merchreel@`, `ossengine@`, `mobile@`, `tuneforge@`, `deemwar@`, `builder@`,
`bookproducts@`. Result: **6 of 7 fleet identities â€” including the dominant one with 170
commits â€” slip through as human and get the lighter, configurable bar.**

This is the product's headline failure mode, found on our own input: an autonomous-agent PR
getting the benefit of the doubt. The fix is to broaden the bundled deemwar entry to the
fleet's `*@deemwar.com` lines (excluding the human `admin@`/owner addresses).

## Finding 2 â€” out-of-the-box detection covers *public* agents, not *your* fleet (HIGH, onboarding)

*source*, the wrong path for a consumer. The right path (`policy.agentAuthors` in config)
should be the #1 step in `mergegate init` and the README quickstart. **This is the single
onboarding step that decides whether the gate actually fires for a given org.**
